Delete Account Request

This page explains how users of Payvand App can request account deletion and removal of associated data.

How to Request Deletion

Email us at: shuhratrahmonov146@email.com

Subject: Delete My Account

Include your registered email address in the message.

What Will Be Deleted

  • Your account profile
  • Login access
  • Personal account information
  • Associated app data where applicable

What May Be Retained

Some records may be retained when required for legal, security, fraud prevention, or accounting obligations.

Deletion Time

Requests are processed within 30 business days.
